Opportunity Attack

If a player or encounter moves away from a player or encounter, it triggers an Opportunity Attack.

In 5e, this is a Reaction used to make one melee attack against a visible enemy that willingly moves out of your reach (typically 5 ft).

  • The attack occurs right before the target leaves your range.
  • You cannot use this if the enemy uses the Disengage action.
  • You cannot use this if the enemy is moved involuntarily (e.g., pushed, dropped, or teleported).

Ready an Action

  • Reaction Cost: Holding an action uses your Reaction for this round.
  • Concentration: If you Ready a script/spell, you must maintain concentration on it until released.
  • Expiration: If the trigger doesn't occur before your next turn, the action is lost.
