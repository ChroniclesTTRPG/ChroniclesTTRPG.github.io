Codex Definitions

Arcane Script

A magical spell or incantation cast by magic users. Requires specific components, activations, and falls under a school of magic.

Combat Feature

A mundane or extraordinary physical ability, technique, or tactical maneuver used by martial classes. Does not require magical components.

Tricks (0)

Minor scripts that can be cast repeatedly without expending magical energy or slots. Also known as Cantrips.

V, S, M (Components)

V (Verbal): Requires spoken words. S (Somatic): Requires free use of at least one hand. M (Material): Requires specific physical objects.

Duration

How long the effect lasts. 'Instantaneous' means the effect happens immediately and leaves no lingering magical aura.
